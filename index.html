Project Overview

PowerSync is a smart energy monitoring and load control system developed for the Technology Transfer and Business Development Office (TTBDO) of Saint Mary’s University. The project focuses on real-time energy visibility, secure control, and actionable insights aligned with ISO 50001 energy management standards.

Problem Statement

University facilities lacked a centralized way to monitor energy consumption, identify inefficiencies, and control electrical loads remotely. Manual monitoring resulted in delayed responses, higher energy costs, and limited data for decision-making.

Data Visualization

A web-based dashboard presents daily and weekly statistics for informed energy management.
